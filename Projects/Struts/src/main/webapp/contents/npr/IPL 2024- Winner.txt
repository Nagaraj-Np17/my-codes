IPL 2024 Winner 
The highly anticipated IPL 2024 is about to come to an end after more than two months, with a thrilling final between Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) and Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) at Chennai’s  MA Chidambaram Stadium on Sunday, 26 May. With 9 victories and 3 defeats from 14 games, KKR was able to make a position at the top of the points table for the first time. 

They proceeded to defeat Sunrisers Hyderabad in a one-sided Qualifier 1 match by eight wickets. Kolkata stormed to victory with 8 wickets and 38 balls remaining at the famous  Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, knocking the Orange Army for 159. 

Sunrisers Hyderabad was forced to take a more circuitous route to the finals following the disaster in Qualification 1. At Chennai’s  MA Chidambaram Stadium on Friday, they took on the Rajasthan Royals (RR) in Qualifier 2. 

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are predicted by the astrologers to win the match, but the outcome will only be known during the championship final on Sunday, May 26 at  M.A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai at 7:30 p.m. IST.
